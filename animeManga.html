Watching Anime and Reading Manga



Watching anime and reading manga always let me feel relax. I like to read story, it lets me have the chance to experience the life which totally different from mine. It also gives me a lot of inspiration to create my artworks. I can accept various types of anime and manga, such as comedy, romantic, fighting, adventure, emotional and many more. Every author have their own mindset, they always create something new and interesting that beyond my expect, it makes me keep looking forward to their new works.

Anime

Anime is easier to watch because it contains motion, music, character's voice and others. I like to watch action anime because it always contains high quality battle scene that make me watch intently. Besides, emotional anime is also my favourite. It always contains the wonderful face emotional expression that reality actor's acting cannot replace.




Manga (Comic)

I also very like to read manga because I am enjoyable in admiring the hand drawing of cartoonist. The cartoonists apply only back and white color in the content of manga, but their artworks look very vivid and pretty. I read romantic style manga the most because I don't want to feel so stress when reading a manga.

Drawing and Creating Stories



I started drawing since I'm was a small kid. At the begining, I just drawing for fun, I drew a lot of things without thinking. The moment that I realised the purpose of my drawing is because of my brother. A long time ago, I looked at my brother's artwork, and he was explaining the personality and the story of the characters that he created, it was very attractive to me. From that time, I started created a lot of stories and drawing characters for my stories. It becomes the motion for me to draw gradually. Thanks to this wonderful opportunity, it lets me found the thing I like and my speciality.








 I like to create sad story but cover with hope. The favourite thing when I create a story is to create my characters' emotional. When characters are standing in different position, according to their personality, they will taking what kind of action to face their challenges? I always enjoying in this part of creation. Most of my characters are waiting to be rescue by others, because of all characters that I created are containing my personality and thinking, when I redeem them in my stories, I feel that I am be rescuing too.



Although my artworks are still in low completion, but I hope that they will become end product with high quality some day. Maybe they will be more vivid or be colored, or even can moving in an animation production. I will keep work hard to let the dream come true.

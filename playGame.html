Playing Mobile Game



Fate/Grand Order is my favourite mobile game. I already playing it for 1253 days (11.1.2021). Everytime I feel shock when I look at the playing time duration. I still playing it because it brings me a lot of memories and emotion, which cause me cannot abandon it easily. I know it sound weird, but the characters inside this game are just like my family member, they accompany with me and help me across a lot of hardship in my life. Hope that I will never forget this wonderful feeling in my future.



I like the most in Fate/Grand Order is its story and character's design. The story is about the player takes the role of "Master" and commands a group of individuals called "Servants," who are typically historical, literary, and mythological figures from various cultures, to different country in different century to save the world. The story is depth but joyful, and covered with full of heroic sense. Its characters are also designed in gorgeous costumes with different cultural concepts, and characters' personality are designed perfectly to hander their historical stories.
